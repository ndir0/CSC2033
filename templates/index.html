Obesity is an increasingly common problem

Our mission is to raise awareness and understanding of obesity. We aim to achieve this by providing information through our interactive website. According to the World Health Organization, 25% of the world population are overweight. It is important that we reduce this statistic.

Sustainable Development Goals

The Sustainable Development Goals or Global Goals are a collection of 17 interlinked global goals designed to be a "blueprint to achieve a better and more sustainable future for all". The SDGs were set up in 2015 by the United Nations General Assembly and are intended to be achieved by the year 2030. Obesity falls under one of their goals. Therefore, it is crucial that we help to accomplish this.
