A Guide to Healthy Eating

Overview

Eating a healthy, balanced diet is vital to maintaining both physical and mental health. It is important that we follow a guide so we do not fall into any misconceptions or misleading information.

Fruits and Vegetables

The general guideline is that we should consume five portions of a variety of fruits and vegetables. They are easy to consume as they are not dense in calories. Also, there are different methods in consuming them such as creating smoothies. This is recommended as they are a great source of vitamins and minerals. Additionally, they are fantastic alternatives to snacks.

Starchy Foods

Starchy foods are our main source of carbohydrates and play an important role in a healthy diet. There are many varieties of starchy foods. One example is wholemeal which contain more vitamins and minerals than junk food.

Milk and Dairy

Foods within this category are great sources of protein. This is essential if your goal is to put on muscle as well as lose weight. Additionally, these also contain certain vitamins and minerals that are extremely beneficial such as calcium.

Foods High in Protein

This includes foods such as fish, eggs, meat etc. Similar to the previous category, protein us an important macronutrient. This helps our body to grow and repair itself which is essential when exercising as our bodies need protein to help adjust to change.
